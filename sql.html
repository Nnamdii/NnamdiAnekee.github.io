Mastering SQL: Unleashing the Power of Data Retrieval and Manipulation

In the world of data, Structured Query Language (SQL) is the key that unlocks the vast treasure troves of information stored in databases. From crafting intricate queries to optimizing database performance, SQL is the backbone of efficient data management.
Here, you'll find a showcase of my SQL projects, each one a testament to my proficiency in harnessing the potential of SQL. These projects demonstrate:
  • Data Retrieval: My ability to extract valuable insights from databases using complex SQL queries.
  • Data Manipulation: How I can reshape and clean data, making it ready for analysis and reporting.
  • Database Design: The art of creating efficient database structures to support business needs.
  • Performance Tuning: Techniques to optimize database speed and efficiency.
I invite you to explore the following SQL projects, each a unique journey through the world of structured data. Whether you're a fellow data enthusiast or someone seeking data-driven solutions, I hope my work inspires and educates.




  • Covid 19 EDA

    In the world of data exploration, this SQL-based exploratory data analysis (EDA) on COVID-19 data was a remarkable journey into the heart of a global pandemic. The project employed two key tables - COVID-19 deaths and COVID-19 vaccinations - as the foundation for an in-depth exploration. By meticulously converting and altering these tables, I transformed the data into a format that allowed for the manipulation of essential data points.
    The insights gained from this endeavor were nothing short of extraordinary. They ranged from mortality rates and the number of cases at the country level, offering a close-up perspective, to infection rates at the continental level, providing a broader, "zoom-out" effect. It was eye-opening to discover that certain countries had remained virtually COVID-free. The integration of these two data sources, achieved through the utilization of Common Table Expressions (CTEs), temporary tables, and window functions, allowed me to unveil the intricate relationship between the total number of infected individuals and the corresponding number of vaccinated people across all countries. This discovery shed light on the effectiveness of vaccination efforts worldwide.
    This project exemplified the power of SQL in unraveling the layers of complex data and transforming it into actionable insights. It showcased the dynamic potential that lies within SQL and underscored the importance of data analysis in our ever-changing world, where understanding the numbers can be the key to informed decision-making and global health.

  • Nashville Housing Data Cleaning Using SQL

    In the realm of data preparation, this SQL project shines as a testament to the art of transforming raw, unrefined data into a format primed for meaningful manipulation and analysis. The journey began with the recognition that data, in its natural state, often requires careful reshaping.
    In this endeavor, I undertook the task of standardizing and altering data formats, an essential step towards achieving consistency and usability. Where gaps existed, I employed an eagle-eyed approach, populating empty data points by sourcing information from related data within the same table.
    One of the more intricate aspects of the project was the art of breaking down and separating unruly data from columns that had become inaccessible into usable, structured columns. This was accomplished through the skillful application of SQL functions such as substrings, charindex, and parsename. These techniques allowed for the extraction of crucial insights from otherwise tangled data.
    Inconsistencies within the dataset were addressed with precision, using CASE functions to harmonize the conflicting data. Complex queries and Common Table Expressions (CTEs) further enriched the project, enabling the aggregation of data into a streamlined format. To conclude this comprehensive data overhaul, duplicates were systematically removed, unveiling a dataset that was not only cleaner but also more apt for insightful analysis.
    This project stands as a testament to the power of data transformation in the pursuit of data-driven excellence. It reaffirms that, through careful and methodical data cleaning, we can unlock the full potential of information, making it a valuable asset in decision-making and data exploration.
